The best place for sushi: Eat Tokyo, Soho

Read time: 1 minute

Eat tokyo is a small chain of restaurants with eight branches spread throughout London and three in Germany. The menu features an extensive range of sushi rolls, nigiri sushi, sashimi, tempura, bento boxes, and many full-sized dishes. While it is not a dedicated vegetarian restaurant, the menu is well equipped to cater to vegetarians and vegans.
